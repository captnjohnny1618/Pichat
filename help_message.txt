To send messages to everyone connected, just type your message and
press enter!

>> Hey everyone! How's it going? (enter)

To send a message to one user, type @username message. For
instance, to send a message to user "john" you'd write:

>> @john hey john! How's it going? (enter)

Finally you can get/set information from/on the server via\n
the # symbol:

    #ip      Get your ip and port number
    #away    Change your status to away
    #back    Change your status to online
    #whos    Get a list of who is connected and their status
    #exit    Exit the program
